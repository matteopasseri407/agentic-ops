# 3D Services Commercial Website

## Summary

A public commercial website built for an Italian 3D scanning and design studio. The site connects public-facing presentation (services, materials, company positioning) to operational quote intake handed off into the internal operating system.

Live site: https://me3design.it

## Operations Angle

The build focuses on the marketing-to-quote handoff: a structured public site that does not stop at a generic contact form, but routes prospects into a quote flow connected to the broader internal operating system.

## System Built

- Service pages for scanning, design, production, and materials
- Structured quote flow with intake routing
- FAQ and company presentation
- Multilingual structure
- Motion and visual presentation
- Analytics and error boundary support

## Architecture (Sanitized)

- React, TypeScript, Vite
- Tailwind, Framer Motion
- Three.js, React Three Fiber, Drei (3D presentation)
- React Router
- Vercel Analytics

Analytics IDs and any private operational identifiers are kept private.

## Operating Outcome

- Turned a technical service offering into a structured commercial web experience.
- Created clearer positioning across scanning, design, and production services.
- Connected public presentation to operational quote intake instead of a generic contact form.

Screenshots and a sanitized walkthrough provided on call, with analytics references and operational/private details removed.
